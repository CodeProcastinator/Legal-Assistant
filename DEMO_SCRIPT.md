## TIPS FOR RECORDING

1. **Use good lighting** - face a window or lamp
2. **Clear audio** - quiet room, speak clearly
3. **Screen resolution** - 1920x1080 recommended
4. **Browser** - Use Chrome, hide bookmarks bar
5. **Practice** - Run through once before recording
6. **Pace** - Speak slowly and clearly
7. **Zoom** - Make sure text is readable

## TOOLS

- **Screen recording**: OBS Studio (free) or Loom
- **Editing**: CapCut (free) or DaVinci Resolve
- **Upload**: YouTube (unlisted) or Google Drive
