VENDOR AGREEMENT

This Vendor Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into on January 15, 2024

BETWEEN:

ABC Technologies Pvt. Ltd., a company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, 
having its registered office at 123 Tech Park, Bangalore, Karnataka 560001 
(hereinafter referred to as the "Company")

AND

XYZ Suppliers, a partnership firm having its office at 456 Industrial Area, 
Chennai, Tamil Nadu 600001 (hereinafter referred to as the "Vendor")

WHEREAS the Company desires to engage the Vendor for supply of IT equipment and 
related services, and the Vendor agrees to provide such supplies on the terms 
set forth herein.

NOW THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

1. SCOPE OF SUPPLY
1.1 The Vendor shall supply computer hardware, networking equipment, and 
related accessories as specified in Purchase Orders issued by the Company.
1.2 All goods must meet the specifications provided by the Company.

2. TERM
2.1 This Agreement is effective from January 15, 2024 and shall remain valid 
for a period of two (2) years until January 14, 2026.
2.2 The Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one-year periods 
unless terminated by either party.

3. PRICING AND PAYMENT
3.1 Prices shall be as per Schedule A attached hereto.
3.2 All prices are exclusive of GST which shall be charged at applicable rates.
3.3 Payment shall be made within 7 days of invoice receipt.
3.4 Payment shall be made in advance for orders exceeding Rs. 5,00,000.

4. DELIVERY
4.1 Delivery shall be made to Company's designated locations.
4.2 In case of delay beyond 7 days, the Vendor shall pay penalty of 5% per week 
of the order value, without any maximum cap.

5. LIABILITY
5.1 The Vendor shall be liable for all damages whatsoever arising from or in 
connection with the supply of goods, including indirect and consequential damages.
5.2 There shall be no limitation on the Vendor's liability under this Agreement.

6. INDEMNIFICATION
6.1 The Vendor shall indemnify and hold harmless the Company from any and all 
claims, losses, damages, liabilities, and expenses (including attorney fees) 
arising from or related to the Vendor's performance or non-performance.

7. TERMINATION
7.1 The Company may terminate this Agreement at any time without prior notice 
at its sole discretion.
7.2 The Vendor may terminate with 90 days prior written notice.
7.3 Upon termination, all pending orders shall be fulfilled without payment.

8. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
8.1 All intellectual property rights in any customizations, modifications, or 
developments shall vest exclusively with the Company, including any pre-existing 
IP of the Vendor used in such work.

9. CONFIDENTIALITY
9.1 The Vendor shall maintain confidentiality of all Company information in 
perpetuity, even after termination of this Agreement.

10. WARRANTY
10.1 All products and services are accepted on an AS-IS basis without any 
express or implied warranties of merchantability or fitness for purpose.

11. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
11.1 This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Singapore.
11.2 Any disputes shall be resolved through arbitration in Singapore under 
Singapore International Arbitration Centre rules.

12. NON-COMPETE
12.1 The Vendor shall not engage in any business competing with the Company 
or supply similar products to Company's competitors for a period of 3 years 
after termination of this Agreement.

13. AUDIT RIGHTS
13.1 The Company shall have the right to audit the Vendor's records, books, 
and premises at any time without prior notice.

14. AMENDMENT
14.1 The Company reserves the sole right to modify or amend any terms of this 
Agreement at its discretion.
